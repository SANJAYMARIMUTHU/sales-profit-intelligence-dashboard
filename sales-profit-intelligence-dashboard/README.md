\# 📊 Sales \& Profit Intelligence Dashboard



\## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides comprehensive insights into sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, and future revenue forecasting.



The dashboard is designed to help business stakeholders monitor KPIs, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions.



\---



\## Objectives



\- Analyze overall sales and profit performance

\- Track monthly revenue and profit trends

\- Identify top-performing products and customers

\- Evaluate category and segment performance

\- Monitor business growth metrics

\- Forecast future sales trends



\---
